Sensors

A Sensor converts the physical parameter (for example: temperature, blood pressure, humidity, speed, etc.) into a signal which can be measured electrically.Based on the output the sensors may be classified as Digital and Analog Sensors.


How do you choose a sensor

  • Accuracy
  • Environmental Condition
  • Range
  • Calibration
  • Resolution
  • Cost
  • Precision
