⚙️ Parameters (adjustable)

BBG RV (Beitragsbemessungsgrenze Rentenversicherung): Maximum income subject to pension & unemployment insurance contributions. Also determines bAV tax-free limit (8%) and SV-free limit (4%).
BBG KV (Beitragsbemessungsgrenze Krankenversicherung): Maximum income subject to health & long-term care insurance contributions.
RV = Rentenversicherung (pension), KV = Krankenversicherung (health), PV = Pflegeversicherung (long-term care), AV = Arbeitslosenversicherung (unemployment). Rates shown are total (employer + employee); each pays half.
Höchstbeitrag knappschaftl. RV: Upper limit for Rürup/Basisrente deduction (§10 Abs. 3 EStG). Your Rürup room = this value minus your total RV contributions.

👶 Childcare Costs (Krippe/Kita)

80% of childcare costs (excluding meals) are deductible, max €4,800 per child per year (§10 Abs. 1 Nr. 5 EStG). Only for children under 14.
