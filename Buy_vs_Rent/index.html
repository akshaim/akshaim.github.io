🏡  5. Owner Monthly Housing Costs

These costs apply to the buyer only. Both buyer and renter pay their own utilities (Nebenkosten/Betriebskosten) separately, so only the owner-specific costs below are included in the comparison.

Hausgeld / WEG Costs (non-recoverable)
€ /mo
Owner's share of building mgmt, Rücklage (reserve fund), common areas. Typical €2–4/m²/mo.
Grundsteuer (Property Tax)
€ /mo
Post-2025 reform (Bayern): Äquivalenzbetrag × Hebesatz. Example — 75 m² unit with ~90 m² land share: Äquivalenzbetrag ≈ 75×0.31 + 90×0.04 = 26.85 € → annual tax ≈ €91 (340% Hebesatz) to €161 (600%) / ~€8–13/mo. Typical Bavarian cities at ~490% aufkommensneutral: ≈ €11–14/mo. Check your Grundsteuerbescheid or Finanzamt for the exact figure — it has dropped significantly from the old calculation for most apartments.

€ /mo
Owner's personal maintenance reserve for the unit (e.g. kitchen, bathroom). ~€1/m²/mo typical. In addition to WEG Rücklage above.

Adjustment Options
Deduct property sale costs from buy-path wealth (~4.5% of sale price: Makler buyer+seller ~3.57% + Notar ~0.75% + Grundbuch ~0.25%)
Apply Abgeltungsteuer on ETF gains (26.375% × 70% = ~18.5% effective) — 30% Teilfreistellung for equity ETFs (§20 EStG). Property gain (primary residence, §23 Abs. 1 Nr. 1 S. 3 EStG): tax-free after ~3 calendar years of owner-occupation.
Show wealth chart in real (inflation-adjusted) euros — all values deflated to today's purchasing power
🏦 Property capital gains — Eigennutzungsausnahme (§23 Abs. 1 Nr. 1 Satz 3 EStG): Since we are comparing buying your own home vs renting the same apartment, the owner-occupancy exception applies. Capital gains are fully tax-free if you lived in the property during the year of sale and the two preceding calendar years (i.e. ~3 calendar years of Eigennutzung — in practice potentially as few as ~13 months if occupation spans parts of 3 calendar years). No minimum holding period applies — the 10-year Spekulationsfrist is only for non-owner-occupied (rented-out / investment) properties. Property equity is therefore shown without any capital gains tax deduction throughout this calculator.
