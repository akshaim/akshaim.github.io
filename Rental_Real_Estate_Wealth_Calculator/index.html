📊  7. Rental Yield — Tax Deductions (Year 1 Analysis)
📄 Find your zvE in your Steuerbescheid (Finanzamt).
Look for: „Für die Anwendung des ESt-Tarifs maßgebender Betrag" — the amount after all personal deductions (Sonderausgaben, Vorsorgeaufwendungen, Kinderfreibeträge etc.), used directly as the base for the §32a EStG tax formula.

💡 No Steuerbescheid to hand? As a rough guide, zvE ≈ 75–85% of your gross salary (Bruttolohn) for a typical employee — the gap covers social insurance contributions and the Arbeitnehmer-Pauschbetrag. Use a lower percentage if you have higher deductions (e.g. PKV, high Werbungskosten).

✅ §7(5a) + §7b can be combined — confirmed by official EStG text:
§7b Abs. 1 EStG explicitly reads: „…neben der Absetzung für Abnutzung nach § 7 Absatz 4 oder 5a…"
This means §7b Sonder-AfA may be claimed alongside either §7(4) linear AfA or §7(5a) degressive AfA.

⚠️ §7b energy standard for post-2022 Bauantrag (§7b Abs. 2 Nr. 2 EStG):
Buildings with Bauantrag after 31.12.2022 must meet Effizienzhaus 40 mit Nachhaltigkeits-Klasse and prove it via the QNG (Qualitätssiegel Nachhaltiges Gebäude) quality label. EH55 does not qualify — only EH40 + QNG meets the threshold. Herstellungskosten must not exceed €5,200/m²; deduction base is capped at €4,000/m².

RE Advantage vs ETF
—

ETF method: Same equity invested day 1. Each year the net real estate cash flow (rent + tax saving − mortgage payments − running costs) is mirrored: if RE costs you money, the ETF investor also invests that amount; if RE pays you, the ETF investor withdraws it. ETF gains taxed at ~18.5% effective (Abgeltungssteuer 26.375% × 70% Teilfreistellung for equity funds). RE capital gain is fully tax-free after a 10-year hold (§23 EStG). Comparison is indicative — Vorabpauschale, currency risk and sequence-of-returns risk not modelled.
